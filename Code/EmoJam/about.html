About Us

The Techno Tribe

Hello everyone, my name is Tahseen Begum and I'm the leader of this group. We're a team of four individuals who have come together to work on a project for our semester project.
FaceMelody is an innovative web application that combines facial emotion detection with song recommendations. By analyzing the user's facial expressions in real-time, it provides personalized song suggestions that match their emotional state. The provided code defines the structure and styling of the web application, as well as the JavaScript code for updating the song recommendations dynamically. With FaceMelody, users can discover new music that resonates with their emotions and enhances their listening experience. Throughout our time working together, we aim to maintain open communication, encourage creativity, and uphold a positive and supportive team environment. We are students from K L University, Hyderabad. We are BTech students from CSE branch (Honors).
